About This Archive

CASE FILE ARCHIVES is an independent paranormal research archive created by Nitesh Hemnani.

The archive documents historically significant paranormal reports through structured research, archival review, witness testimony, investigative records, and skeptical analysis.

Each case file aims to present documented evidence alongside alternative explanations, allowing readers to examine the material from multiple perspectives.

This project does not claim definitive proof of paranormal phenomena. Its purpose is educational, historical, and research-oriented.

Research Principles

  • Documented sources whenever possible
  • Balanced presentation of claims and skepticism
  • Distinction between fact and interpretation
  • Archival preservation of historical cases
