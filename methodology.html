Research Methodology & Standards

CASE FILE ARCHIVES aims to document historical paranormal cases through structured research and archival review.

Sources Used

  • Witness testimony
  • Investigator reports
  • Newspaper archives
  • Books and published research
  • Audio, photographic, and documentary records

Research Principles

  • Present both paranormal and skeptical viewpoints.
  • Avoid claims of definitive proof.
  • Distinguish documented facts from interpretation.
  • Use multiple independent sources whenever possible.
  • Maintain a balanced and objective approach.

Disclaimer

This archive is intended for educational, historical, and research purposes. Inclusion of a case does not imply endorsement of paranormal claims.
